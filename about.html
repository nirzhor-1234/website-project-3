Lamborghini Diablo GTR Engine

Engine

The Lamborghini Diablo is powered by a 6.0-litre V12 engine. This GTR is street legal with revised fuel and ignition systems. This engine has individual throttle bodies, a dynamic air intake duct system, and various other advancments. With this engine, the Diablo can go 338 km/h

Diablo GTR Production

At first there were meant to be only 30 GTR's made. Later after realizing racecars get wrecked, 10 more were made. In total only 40 Diablo GTR's were actually built.

Iconic Predecessor

The Lamborghini Countach is the iconic predecessor of the Lamborghini Diablo. Often this supercar is not seen from how efficient of a racecar it is.

How many Diablo GTRS are in the USA?

There are only 10 known Diablo GTRS in the USA.
